Who we are

We are a group of experienced and skillfull email marketing experts; our expertise has been used from startups to fortune 100, fortune 500 and DAX companies (40 major German blue chip companies trading on the Frankfurt Stock Exchange). What we do

We take care of all your email marketing needs: design graphics to fit your branding, develop custom HTML email templates, groom your email lists, segment your audience(s), keep your email domain reputation clean, do copywriting, create A/B tests, create automation flows, deploy campaigns, analyze campaign(s) performance, develop strategies for your upcoming campaigns and much more.
